About jm_space

We are a collective of engineers, designers, and enthusiasts dedicated to pushing the boundaries of amateur rocketry. Our mission is to combine rigorous engineering practices with innovative design to achieve flight performance that rivals professional systems.

From initial concept through successful launch, every project represents a commitment to precision, safety, and excellence. We believe that advanced model rocketry is the perfect intersection of physics, engineering, and creative problem-solving.
